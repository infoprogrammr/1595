Task : Odd times Occuring Numbers

You are given an array of integers a of length n, There exist only two elements in this array that are occuring odd number of times . For example a = 1,1,2,2,6,7   output=6 7

You need to find those two numbers .  

Constraints:

n=6 . All elements in array are positive whole numbers .

Test Cases :

Input : 3 3 1 4 4 2 Output :1 2

Input : 1 1 6 6 7 8  Output : 7 8

Explanation 1:  Here , 1 2 only occurs 1 time which is odd . thus output is 1 2.
